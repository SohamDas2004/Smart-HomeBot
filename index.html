About the Project

This project, developed at the NEXUS workshop, focuses on creating a smart homebot that operates in both manual and autonomous modes. The bot supported dual operational modes: manual and autonomous. - Manual control was facilitated through the Blynk IoT platform, - Autonomous navigation utilized IR sensors for precise movement and obstacle detection.
Additionally, we developed a web server dashboard that provided real-time monitoring of environmental parameters, including temperature, humidity, and air quality.

Components Used

DHT11 Sensor

Monitors temperature and humidity levels in real-time.

MQ134 Sensor

Detects air quality and monitors gas presence.

Flame Detector

Identifies fire or heat sources for safety.

IR Sensors

Identifies obstacles for seemless dyanmic movement.

L298N

Controls motor movement.

ESP32

Has 32-bit LX6 processors which handles multitasking efficiently.

9V Batteries

Provies power to the motors and sensors.

DC Motors

Provies rotatary power to the wheels.

What can it do for YOU?

Home Cleaning Assistance: With its vacuum cleaner attachment, the homebot can function as an autonomous cleaning assistant, keeping floors tidy without manual effort. It can operate at scheduled times or on-demand, helping to maintain cleanliness with minimal intervention—ideal for busy households or larger living spaces.

Dual Mode Operation (Manual and Autonomous): Through the Blynk IoT platform, you can control the bot manually from your smartphone, making it easy to direct it to specific areas or use it interactively. The autonomous mode lets the bot navigate your home independently using IR sensors, which are especially helpful when you’re away or wish for uninterrupted operation.

Data Dashboard for Real-Time Monitoring: The bot’s web server dashboard displays real-time data on temperature, humidity, and air quality, accessible from any connected device. This feature empowers you to monitor your home’s conditions remotely, receive alerts, and make informed decisions for maintaining a comfortable and safe indoor environment.
